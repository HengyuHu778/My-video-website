About Me

My name is Hengyu Hu, and I come from China. I am a humorous and optimistic person. I enjoy playing basketball and video games, but what I love the most is traveling and filming. I like to document life, whether through photos or videos.

I also have some experience in film production, working in a team where everyone took on different roles. I believe that each department—such as directing, screenwriting, cinematography, sound, art design, and post-production—plays a vital role in creating a complete film. It is fascinating to see how everyone’s efforts come together into one final piece, and it always brings me a strong sense of pride.

What’s more, working in a group is never just about the film itself—there are always stories and experiences shared within the team that make the process even more special. That is why I am really looking forward to collaborating with my classmates in this program to create meaningful and inspiring works in the future.
